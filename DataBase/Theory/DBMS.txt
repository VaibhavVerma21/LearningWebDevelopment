Myth: mySQL, Oracle, MongoDB are not database but database management systems.
DBMS -> Database Management Systems 

DBMS has two parts - the actual database and the management layer(systems).
Management layer is just software, normal code/program which manages the data.

DBMS ensures security, prevents inefficiency and is easy to use.



Classifications of DBMS -

1. Based on data models -
Relational, Hierarchical, Network, Object based.

2. Based on number of user -
Single, Multiple

3. Based on distribution of data -
Centralized, Distributed
